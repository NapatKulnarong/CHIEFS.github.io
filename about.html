About Us

We teach things that
school doesn't teach you.

CHIEFS was found in 2023 by Napat Kulnarong. Our first campus is in London, UK. At CHIEFS, we teach skills that school doesn't teach you, skills that are essential in the 21st century: leadership, critical thinking, communication skills, public speaking, collaboration, social skills, social responsibility, civic literacy, and global awareness. Because only the young can run the world, we need to provide them with all of these skills. Who knows? One day they might be the one who changes the world.
