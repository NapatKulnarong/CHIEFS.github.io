Activities

Model United Nations

Model United Nations is a simulation of the United Nations Conference to resolve a given situation or crisis. Each student will play the role of country representative. This activity gives students skills in analyzing, criticizing, and ecpressing opinions on various topics.

Running A Campaign

Students will work together to do a researh about problems that are currently happening in our society or in the world, then choose 1 topic to run a campaign. For example, they might run a campaign to educate people about marriage equality e.g. make a video, give away a brochure.

Leaders Talk

Socrates once said "Smart people learn from everything and everyone, average people learn from their experiences, stupid people already have all the answers.", To provide student experiences from various fields, CHIEFS will invite successful persons from around the world to give students a speech.
